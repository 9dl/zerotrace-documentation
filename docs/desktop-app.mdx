## ‚úÖ Supported Devices

ZeroTrace Desktop works with:
- **ZeroTrace Kit**

<Callout type="info">
    <b>How to check your device:</b><br/>
    Look for two USB-C ports on your device one labeled <b>COM</b> (or <b>UART</b>), and one labeled <b>USB</b>.<br/>
    The <b>COM port</b> is for connecting to the desktop app.<br/>
    The <b>USB port</b> is for payload injection only.
</Callout>
