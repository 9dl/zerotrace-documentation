---
title: 🖱️ Mouse Commands
---

ZeroTrace can **control the mouse cursor** for advanced HID automation and testing.
- 📍 **Absolute positioning**: Move the cursor to specific screen coordinates
- ↔️ **Relative movement**: Shift the cursor from its current position
- 🏴‍☠️ **Jitter mode**: Add random movements for human-like behavior
- 🔄 **Click automation** for left, right, and middle buttons
- 🖱️ **Scroll wheel emulation** for vertical scrolling
- 🔁 **Combine with keyboard/gamepad scripts** for complex payloads
