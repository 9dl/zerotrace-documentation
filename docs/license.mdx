## ğŸ›  License Activation Process

### For New Devices

1. **Locate Your Key**:
 - Check your email receipt
 - Find it in your account dashboard after registration
 - Physical card included with some purchases

2. **Activate via Device Web Interface**:
 - Power on your ZeroTrace device
 - Connect to its WiFi network (e.g., `Phone X ZT`)
 - Open [`192.168.4.1`](http://192.168.4.1) in your browser
 - Navigate to **License Activation**
 - Enter your exact license key (case-sensitive, no spaces)
 - Select **Activate**

### For Dashboard Users

- **Automatic Activation**:
If your device is pre-activated, your license will appear in your dashboard automatically no manual entry needed.
- **Manual Activation**:
If prompted, you can enter the license key shown in your dashboard directly in the device web interface.

<Callout type="success">
    ğŸ‰ **Dashboard Users:** When you register a purchased device, your license will be automatically linked to your account and device no manual entry needed for pre-activated units.
</Callout>
